How to Book

Please send an email to helloinkmanager@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your age
  • Your instagram handle
  • Tattoo artist’s name and instagram handle
  • Dates you are available
  • Is your tattoo flash or custom?
  • Tattoo description: including color, placement, left or right, and size
  • Reference images
  • Full booking only or include in-person interpretation?
  • Any other questions you have for us or your artist
We'll reply within 1–2 business days to confirm availability.
