Client Policies

Age Requirement

  • We only work with clients who are 19 years old or older.
Full Booking Service
  • Full payment must be made before we contact any artists.
  • If your chosen artist is unavailable or cancels, we offer one free rebooking attempt with a second artist. A third attempt will count as a new booking session.
  • If you cancel and wish to switch artists mid-process, each new artist will also count as a new booking session.
  • Booking fees are per person. For group sessions, each client must pay a separate booking fee.
  • Communication with artists is available for up to 1 week after the session.
In-Person Interpretation
  • Interpretation is only available if your booking was made through Hello Ink and your artist consents to having a translator present.
  • Pre-payment is required. We calculate hours based on the artist’s time estimate.
  • Refunds are not available even if the session ends early.
  • If the session runs longer than expected, extra fees must be paid afterward via Wise or in cash (KRW). No change available.
  • All sessions are billed hourly, with a 10-minute grace period. Additional time beyond that is charged in 30-minute blocks.
  • Interpretation is only available until 7:00 p.m.
  • For groups getting the same design: up to 3 people are covered by the base fee. For 4 or more, an additional 1-hour fee is charged per person.
  • Sessions with different tattoo designs are considered separate bookings.
  • For clients getting multiple small tattoos in one session, the booking fee remains the same (1 session), but in-person interpretation will be counted as a minimum of 2 hours, as it requires continuous support throughout.
  • For tattoos requiring multiple sessions, the initial booking fee covers coordination for the entire project. However, in-person interpretation is charged separately for each session day.
Refunds & Cancellations
  • If we cancel, a full refund is issued.
  • If you cancel before we contact any artists, we can refund your payment.
  • After artist contact begins, refunds are no longer available.
  • No-shows will result in being ineligible for future services.
Artist Deposits
  • You can send your artist’s deposit through us for convenience.
  • We charge a ₩7,500 or $5 service fee to cover processing and currency conversion.
Artist Selection
  • We don’t choose tattoo artists for clients.
  • You choose your artist. We help you communicate and handle the process smoothly.
  • Our role is to coordinate bookings, provide communication support, and offer in-person help if needed.
Disclaimer
  • We are not responsible for the tattoo quality, healing process, or anything the artist says or does during your session.
Payments
  • We accept payments via Wise (USD) or cash (KRW). PayPal and credit cards are not currently supported.
  • If you live in Korea and have a Korean bank account, we can provide our account info upon request.
Punctuality
  • Please arrive on time. Clients who arrive more than 15 minutes late without notice will be charged a $30 (₩45,000) late fee.
  • If they are more than 30 minutes late, the translator may leave and no refunds will be issued—regardless of prior notice. We appreciate your understanding and respect for our time.
  • Please note: Some tattoo artists have a 15-minute late policy and may cancel the session after that time. If this happens, it is considered out of our hands and no refunds will be issued.
